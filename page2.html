AMAZING CONCEPT

Lamborghini Concept

Lamborghini is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963. The company is known for producing high-performance, exotic cars that are often recognized for their sleek designs and powerful engines.

The Terzo Millennio anticipates the future and projects the emotions of super sports cars into the electrical revolution era. This design concept car features futuristic lines and is designed with the goal of maximizing aerodynamic performance, stylistic pursuit and driving emotions through advanced technological solutions including an energy storage system based on super capacitators, functional materials in carbon fiber and electric propulsion true to the spirit of a super sports car.

The Lamborghini Terzo Millennio is a groundbreaking concept car that represents the Italian automaker's vision for the future of high-performance electric vehicles. Unveiled in 2017, the Terzo Millennio, which translates to "Third Millennium" in English, is a collaboration between Lamborghini and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). One of its key innovations is the use of electric propulsion, incorporating cutting-edge energy storage systems and advanced materials. The Terzo Millennio's carbon fiber structure is designed to store electrical energy, essentially turning the entire body of the car into a power storage system. Additionally, the concept features advanced technologies like self-healing materials to detect and repair cracks, providing a glimpse into Lamborghini's forward-thinking approach to both performance and sustainability in the automotive industry. Although the Terzo Millennio is a concept car and not currently in production, its futuristic design and innovative technologies showcase Lamborghini's commitment to pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering.
